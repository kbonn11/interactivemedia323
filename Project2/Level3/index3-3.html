When all the
golden birds

fly home across the
blue deep water;

On shore I sit rapt in its
scattering glitter;

departure rustles through
the trees.

This farewell is

a
s

and
separation

My head on my arm
I fall asleep easily.

On my eyes a mother's breath,
from her mouth
to my heart.

sleep, child, and dream
now the sun is gone.

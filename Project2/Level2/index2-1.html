1. When all
    • the golden birds
    • fly home across
      • the blue deep water;
  2. On shore
    • I sit
      • rapt in its
      • scattering glitter;
    • departure rustles
      • through the trees
  3. This farewell
    • is vast
      • and separation
    • draws close
    • but reunion
      • that also is certain
  4. My head
    • on my arm
    • I fall asleep easily.
  5. On my eyes
    • a mother's breath,
      • from her mouth
    • to my heart:
  6. sleep,
    • child,
    • and dream
    • now the sun is gone.
